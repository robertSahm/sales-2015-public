Lucera Connect: Modern connectivity managment for the modern financial world

How Lucera Connect works

Lucera Connect elevates the network to the application level: Each connection is specific to a particular application. We call these Logical Flows (L-Flows).

Lucera Connect takes the hard work out of networking. Your network no longer looks like a collection of fiber lines and bandwidth restrictions, rather a simple list of active connections that help you do business globally.

With Lucera Connect you will:

Accelerate your time-to-market

Enable your team to spin up and down connections in a fraction of time required by legacy providers.

Dramatically lower your costs

No charge for bandwidth or transcontinental fiber.

Empower your business

Quickly onboard clients, connect to liquidity destinations and expand your business reach through the user driven portal.

See through network costs

Scale your network connectivity month-to-month, with no long term commitment

Simplify your network

One physical cross connect to any Lucera location enables access to the entire Lucera Connect network. Provisioning is driven by you via the Web-based portal.
